The Chronic Care Crisis: Fragmented, Reactive, Invisible

Healthcare systems today manage chronic diseases reactively. Patients slip through care gaps. Care teams burn out from administrative burden and manual workflows. Clinical data stays siloed. And the result? Rising HbA1c, medication non-compliance, preventable complications, and missed revenue—all while bearing the cost of readmissions.

Fragmentation

Patient data scattered across EHRs, monitoring devices, and care teams

Burnout & Inefficiency

Care coordinators spending 60% of time on admin instead of patient care

Invisibility

No real-time visibility into patient adherence, outcomes, or risk until it's a crisis

40% of healthcare costs are for managing chronic disease, yet outcomes remain unchanged

Care Management Platform: The Command Center for Chronic Care

Coordinate chronic care with unified views, automated workflows, and audit-ready documentation across CCM and RPM. The platform supports clinical delivery, operational efficiency, compliance, and revenue capture in one system that integrates with your existing electronic health record.

Patient Enrollment and Risk Stratification

Automated identification and enrollment of eligible patients from your EHR. AI-powered risk segmentation helps care teams prioritize highest urgency interventions. Configurable workflows adapt to specialties including diabetes, heart failure, COPD and more.

Enterprise benefit

Minimize manual screening and standardize enrollment workflows for measurable operational gains.

Clinical Workflows and Monitoring

Pre-configured clinical pathways proven to improve engagement and adherence. Integrations with connected devices, manual data entry, telehealth visits and patient-reported outcomes come together in one workflow. Real-time dashboards provide visibility for care coordinators and clinicians.

Enterprise benefit

Unifies data flows so care teams spend more time on care and less time navigating fragmented tools.

Billing and Compliance

Automated CPT code mapping and billing documentation for RPM, CCM, PCM and related chronic care billing categories. Ensures you capture every legitimate reimbursement opportunity while maintaining compliance with CMS requirements.

Enterprise benefit

Chronic Care Execution, Made Visible

Journey Tracker is a care team workspace that turns long chronic care programs into clear, day-by-day execution. It ensures every patient, task, and follow-up is accounted for across months of care.

What It Does

  • Maps every patient to a structured care journey with defined phases and timelines
  • Auto-surfaces daily tasks across all assigned patients and roles
  • Flags missed, delayed, and upcoming actions in real time
  • Replaces manual tracking across notes, spreadsheets, and disconnected tools

Teams move from reacting to coordinating to executing.

Why It Matters

  • •Fewer missed tasks across long-term programs
  • •Lower manual coordination effort for care teams
  • •Clear ownership for every patient, every day
  • •Predictable execution as programs scale
